PROCESS

Request for a Requirement Document
✔️ If the requirements are clear in your mind, it is a good practice to document that. Refine it and share with us. Unless you are clear, your partner will never be.

✔️ Our team starts with rough sketching after understanding the target user for the app. This is an inhouse activity, before we share the mock ups

Planning Basic Design
✔️ A good product starts with a great design approach to problem solving. Things like how the flow will work, what action controls are given to specific user is covered in the phase.

Development Approach and Technology Selection
✔️ If you have any inclination towards a tech stack or development approach, we will clear all the doubts and finalize that. In this phase we define the platform, technology, environment, structure and framework.

Design the Web App Workflow in Photoshop
✔️ Yes, once we decide on first 4 points, we move to give life to your application. You get to see 2 samples to choose. If you are not satisfied, we will recreate for you. When you select the workflow, we move to another step

UI and Backend Development
✔️ In this phase, our developers work together to create web application’s architecture, design its database structure, develop or customize the modules, and implement all the functionalities mentioned in the scope of work.

QA Testing
✔️ Our QA tester will ensure to break the application. While the developers work on bug fixing/repairing the wall. One of the important phases, to ensure not a single piece of your application is broken - performance testing, functionality testing and automation.

User Acceptance Testing
✔️ This is the last phase of the testing, when you/your team gets their hands on the application. You define a certain criteria for accepting the application; execute and document tests
